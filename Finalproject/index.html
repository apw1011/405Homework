ALAND MOTORS

Welcome to the web site dedicated for the hopes of opening a business, a good business named Aland Motors. This is a car sales organization which promises that all cars for sale have been fixed to as perfect as we could manage from where we found these cars. All of our cars must be restored to close to original shape. We will not sell a car that we have not actually restored.

I hope to begin business with the help of the two cars shown above, both of which resemble cars that I own. The blue 1954 Chevy is fully restored and ready for use. However I did not restore this car, so I will keep it and not sell it. I bought the red Corvette in the year 1999, and it is in good unrestored condition. It needs a spanking, and here, I have the first car sale for Aland Motors.

The investment will come from the trust fund set for me by my parents, and again, at the permission of my parents. I will restore the Vette first, an investment that I am 13000 into. it doesn't need much, yet a fully restored 1974 Corvette is worth near 30000 dollars. I should break even, and the funds will be first allocated in the business. I could use this to locate another investment, a nice used car that I turn into a classic.

This will be the start of my new business. We will buy a wreck, pay to restore it to pristine shape, and sell and ship it for a profit of less than 10% for every car. Some cars will take a loss, and others will bring in a small profit. The profit will be split. Part will go to the workers, and part will go back into the capital, so the business will grow. I decided this figure shall be 25% back into the business. And the rest for Al and everyone who helped out, hence the name, Aland Motors.
