If this is again the case, I will ask for a percentage of cost above the cost as a sales price, which will take no more than 10% home. Then the profit margin will both pay the employees, and strengthen the business. I want to sell all the cars below value.

So this project is in the plan stage of becoming a real business for me. I hope to have great success selling cars.
