I have waited a long time with a dream of selling cars. The cars are fun to buy, and fun to restore, yet it will be more difficult to sell them. I have a plan. I have restored two cars before, and the cost was way less than the ending value for both. If this is again the case, I will ask for a percentage of cost above the cost as a sales price, which will take no more than 10% home. Then the profit margin will both pay the employees, and strengthen the business. I want to sell all the cars below value.
