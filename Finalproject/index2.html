ALAND MOTORS

Aland Motors will be run by me, and I will have friends willing to help me to sell cars. I plan that it should be an LLC after a couple of cars are sold. It is my dream to own some property in or near Manchester, a property where I could build a showroom to house and sell the cars. I would have to sell alot of cars to be able to afford this luxury. So I have to start small, and sell one car at a time. If the venture is profitable, I could restore two at one time. I will be working, so funds can come from my personal account as well.

Buying cars for restoration will be a pleasurable experience. I plan on doing very much research on each car that is in consideration. It is important to analyze the car's condition, so I can know how much it is worth. And then if the price is right, we will buy the car. I plan on searching two markets for the cars that we can restore. I will check the Auto Trader and I will check the Craigs List. And I will look up their values at a site called Hemmings Motor News.
