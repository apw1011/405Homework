An Easy Vegetarian Quesadilla

This is a very easy recipe, and I made it up my self. I have eaten a lot of quesadillas in restaurants, and I asked, "How do they make these?" My friends answered, it's just cheese. "Aah, yes, but what kind of cheese?" They answered, "Shredded!" And I said, "Oh, O.K., that's how." I took it home, and really wanted to do this fact justice.

The Recipe Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 385 degrees.
  2. Place a Valley Bread wrap on a pizza dish.
  3. Spread two cups of Hannaford's Mexican shredded cheese evenly on the wrap.
  4. Spread one cup of brocolli crowns onto the cheese.
  5. Spread one diced plum tomato onto the cheese.
  6. Spread 1/2 cup diced vidalia onion onto the wrap.
  7. Spread one diced Cubanelle pepper onto the wrap.
  8. Spread two more cups of Hannaford's Mexican shredded cheese evenly on top of the wrap.
  9. Place one more Valley Bread wrap on top of the cheese and vegetables and smach it down.
  10. Place in oven for 17 minutes.
  11. When done, slice into eight pieces with a pizza cutter.
  12. Enjoy with any sauce you like.
